Noli Me Tangere Characters

Crisostomo Ibarra

After studying in Europe for seven years, this young and well off man has come back to his home of the Philippines. Due to his father's recent imprisonment and labelling as a heretic, the friars in San Diego, his hometown, now feel they should keep an eye out to him.

Maria Clara

The childhood friend of Ibarra and respected woman in San Diego due to her high social class. Her godfather is Father Damaso who is not fond of Maria and Ibarra's eventual marriage.

Padre Damaso

A Spanish friar who has trouble understanding Tagalog despite staying in Philippines for many years and listening to many people confess their sins to him over those years. He makes false accusations about the nonreligious people who, in his eyes, make him seem less effective as a friar.

Elias

A vagabond revolutionary who dislikes the control that the Spanish government and Catholic church has over the Philippines. Elias promises Ibarra that he will have his protection after he saved his life from a crocodile.

Padre Salvi

A dedicated Spanish friar that replaces Padre Damaso as San Diego's priest. Padre Salvi uses his image to influence politics which benefits himself and the church.
